Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ANATANOWOR ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information obtained through our internal Amazon Advertising integration application (the "Application"). This Application is built solely for our own internal use to manage advertising campaigns on Amazon marketplaces where we operate as a registered seller under the ANATANOWOR brand.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Application is a private, single-organization integration developed for internal advertising operations only. It is not distributed to third parties, not offered as a public service, and not licensed to other advertisers. All data accessed through the Amazon Advertising API is used exclusively for managing the ANATANOWOR brand's own advertising accounts.

3. Information We Access

Through the Amazon Advertising API, our Application accesses the following information from our own Amazon Advertising accounts:

  • Advertising campaign configurations (campaign names, budgets, bids, targeting settings)
  • Ad group and keyword data (keywords, match types, bid amounts)
  • Performance reports (impressions, clicks, spend, sales, ACOS, ROAS)
  • Product advertising data (ASINs, product targeting, search term reports)
  • Account profile information necessary for API authentication

4. How We Use This Information

The information accessed through the Amazon Advertising API is used solely for the following internal business purposes:

  • Monitoring and analyzing the performance of our own advertising campaigns
  • Optimizing keyword bids, budgets, and targeting based on performance data
  • Generating internal reports and dashboards for business decision-making
  • Automating routine campaign management tasks
  • Identifying optimization opportunities and underperforming campaigns

5. Data Storage and Security

We are committed to protecting the security of all data accessed through the Amazon Advertising API:

  • All API credentials (client ID, client secret, refresh tokens) are stored securely using environment variables and are never exposed in source code or public repositories.
  • All data transmission between our Application and Amazon's servers occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Access to the Application and its underlying infrastructure is restricted to authorized internal personnel only.
  • We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose any data accessed through the Amazon Advertising API to any third party.

6. Data Retention

Performance data and reports retrieved from the Amazon Advertising API are retained only for as long as necessary to support our internal advertising operations. Historical data may be retained for trend analysis and year-over-year performance comparisons, in accordance with our internal data management practices.

7. Compliance with Amazon Policies

This Application is designed and operated in full compliance with the Amazon Advertising API License Agreement, the Amazon Ads API Acceptable Use Policy, and all other applicable Amazon developer policies. We do not use the Amazon Advertising API to access or manage any advertising accounts other than those owned by the ANATANOWOR brand.

8. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, transfer, or otherwise disclose any information obtained through the Amazon Advertising API with any third party. The Application is operated entirely for our internal use, and all data remains within our controlled infrastructure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was most recently revised.
